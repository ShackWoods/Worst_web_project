Online Banking

Key impacts of computers on online banking

Enhanced convenience and accessibility:

  • Customers can access their accounts and conduct transactions anytime, anywhere, without visiting a physical branch.
  • Mobile banking apps allow for banking on the go.

Increased speed and efficiency:

  • Transactions are often completed almost instantly.
  • Automation of routine tasks through technologies like AI and machine learning streamlines processes and reduces costs for banks.

Transformation of traditional banking:

  • Banks are reducing their physical branch networks and shifting focus to digital-first models.
  • Competition from digital-only banks (neo-banks) is pushing traditional banks to innovate and adopt new technologies to stay competitive
  • Some banks are transforming remaining branches into centers for financial advice rather than routine transactions.
