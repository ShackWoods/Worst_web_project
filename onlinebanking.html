Online Banking

Computers have revolutionized online banking by enabling 24/7 customer convenience, faster and more efficient transactions, and global accessibility through platforms like mobile apps and websites. They have also led to a shift in traditional banking models, with banks increasingly focusing on digital services, reducing physical branch networks, and using data for personalized offerings. This digital transformation increases competition and introduces new challenges, particularly regarding cybersecurity and the need for continuous adaptation

Key impacts of computers on online banking

Enhanced convenience and accessibility:
Customers can access their accounts and conduct transactions anytime, anywhere, without visiting a physical branch.
Mobile banking apps allow for banking on the go.
